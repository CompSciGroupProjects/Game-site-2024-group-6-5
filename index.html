TicTacToe

1-2 player
TicTacToe is a game where to
players pick spaces to place X's
and O's in a 3x3 grid. The aim of
the game is to get three in a row
and win the game.

Rock Paper Scissors

1 player
Rock Paper Scissors is a game
where two players at the same
time play rock, paper, or scissors.
Rock beats scissors, scissors beats
paper, and paper beats rock.

Connect 4

2 player
Connect 4 is a game where two
players go back and forth placing
chips into the board sliding down
from the top. To win wou must get
4 in a row in any posssible way.
